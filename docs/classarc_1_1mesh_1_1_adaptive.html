Parameters
min_Minimum bound of the mesh.
max_Maximum bound of the mesh.
lights_Vector of lights that may be found within the mesh's bounds.
entities_Vector of entities that may be found within the mesh's bounds.
detectors_Vector of detectors that may be found within the mesh's bounds.
max_depth_Maximum depth the mesh may reach.
target_shapes_Target maximum number of shapes to find within each leaf cell of the mesh.
Precondition
min_.x must be less than max_.x.
min_.y must be less than max_.y.
min_.z must be less than max_.z.
max_depth_ must be positive.
