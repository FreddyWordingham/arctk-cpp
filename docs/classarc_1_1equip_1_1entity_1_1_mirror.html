Construct a mirror enity which has a probability of reflecting incident photons.

Parameters
serial_Serialised wavefront file.
scale_Scaling transformation.
rot_Rotation transformation.
trans_Translation transformation.
front_ref_Reflection probability of the front surface.
back_ref_Reflection probability of the back surface.
Precondition
serial may not be empty.
scale_.x must be positive.
scale_.y must be positive.
