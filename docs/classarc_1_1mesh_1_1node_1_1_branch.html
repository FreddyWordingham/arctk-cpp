Construct a branch node.

Parameters
min_Minimum bound of the node.
max_Maximum bound of the node.
lights_Vector of lights that are found within the node's bounds.
entities_Vector of entities that are found within the node's bounds.
detectors_Vector of detectors that are found within the node's bounds.
cur_depth_Current depth of the node.
max_depth_Maximum depth the mesh may reach.
target_shapes_Target maximum number of shapes to find within each leaf node of the mesh.
Precondition
min_.x must be less than max_.x.
min_.y must be less than max_.y.
min_.z must be less than max_.z.
lights_ shapes must intersect the node.
entities_ shapes must intersect the node.
detectors_ shapes must intersect the node.
max_depth_ must be greater than, or equal to, cur_depth_.
